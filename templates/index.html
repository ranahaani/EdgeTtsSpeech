Frequently Asked Questions

What is VoiceSync?

VoiceSync is a text-to-speech converter that uses Microsoft's Edge TTS technology to generate natural-sounding speech from text. It supports multiple languages and voices.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No, your text and audio files are not stored on our servers. The conversion happens in real-time and the audio is directly sent to your browser for download.

What audio format do I get?

The audio is provided in MP3 format, which is widely supported by most devices and media players.
